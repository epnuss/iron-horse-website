What We Do

Expert Roofing Services

From full replacements to emergency repairs — we handle every roof type South Florida throws at us.

🏠

Tile Roofing

Concrete and clay tile systems built for South Florida's wind loads and hurricane codes. Proper underlayment, mortar, and flashing — done by the book.

🏡

Shingle Roofing

Architectural and impact-rated shingle systems. We work with top manufacturers to ensure your roof passes inspection and lasts decades.

🏗️

Flat Roofing

TPO, modified bitumen, and torch-down systems for flat and low-slope roofs. Waterproof from day one, inspected thoroughly before we leave.

🔧

Roof Repairs

Leaks, missing tiles, blown-off shingles, storm damage — we diagnose fast and fix it right. Emergency response available for active leaks.
